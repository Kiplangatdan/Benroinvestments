About Benro Investments

Benro Investments Limited is a fully registered kenyan General Building contractor for roads,single and multi-story buildings. The company has the National Construction Authority of Kenya authorization for Building Works , Roads & Water works construction.

Currently the company is active in Kenya where we have undertaken several engineering assignments with several government organs such as National water conservation and pipeline cooperation, Ministry of housing, Ministry of public works, Bureti sub-county water office, Sotik sub-county water office, Kenya Rural Roads Authority and public service commission of Kenya. Most of the projects the company has carried out to date are majorly roads and water works , it hopes to take on a greater number of commercial projects and residential developments in the near future.

History

Benro Invesments Ltd, an Engineering and Construction Company started its operations in the year 2007 as a small company handling general supplies, printing needs and general construction renovations and with its outstanding performance track record and growth, it fully fledged into a limited company in the year 2012, undertaking and managing engineering projects within the infrastructure sector. BIL offers services in the construction of Building projects,roads and water&Sewerage projects and related Civil Engineering projects. We bring to the job, the multi-disciplinary teams with requisite capacity, capability and outstanding track record in undertaking & managing major engineering projects.The company has continued to grow over the years as it works towards its vision of being the "Preferred Infrastructure Development Partner in Kenya".
